Bridge Foundry Operations Guide

What is Bridge Foundry? See bridgefoundry.org.

Bridge Foundry empowers people with technology through teaching and facilitating access, enlarging the community of people who give back and teach others.

Using this Operations Guide

The operations guide contains information to help you:

  • Fundraise for your workshop or Bridge.

  • Receive grants from Bridge Foundry for your workshop or Bridge.

  • Understand different formal titles within Bridge Foundry, including Volunteer Bridge Leader, Independent Community, Chapter, and more.

  • Spend money on events or a Bridge.

If you have further questions, get in touch!
